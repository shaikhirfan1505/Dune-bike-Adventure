Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do I need prior experience to ride an adult quad bike?+
No prior experience is needed. Our expert instructors provide full guidance and training before your Dubai desert quad bike adventure.
2. What is the minimum age to ride adult quad bikes in Dubai?+
Riders must be at least 16 years old. A valid driving license is required for single-seat rides to ensure safety.
3. Are safety gear and helmets provided?+
Yes, we provide all safety gear, including helmets, gloves, and protective clothing, for a secure desert quad biking experience.
4. Can I ride double-seat quad bikes with a friend?+
Absolutely! Our double-seat quad bikes are designed for two adults to enjoy the thrilling Dubai desert ride together.
5. What is the duration of adult quad bike rides?+
Adult quad bike rides typically range from 30 minutes to 2 hours, depending on the package and desert route selected.
6. Are the desert quad bikes suitable for beginners?+
Yes, our adult quad bikes are suitable for all skill levels. Beginners are guided step-by-step by trained instructors for a safe adventure.
7. What happens in case of bad weather during the ride?+
If there are strong winds or rain, your Dubai desert quad bike session will be rescheduled at no extra cost to ensure safety.
8. Can I book adult quad bike rides online in Dubai?+
Yes, bookings can be made easily online or via phone. Walk-ins are also welcome at our desert adventure location.
9. Are photos and videos included in the quad biking experience?+
Yes! We provide complimentary photos and videos for all adult quad bike rides in Dubai to capture your thrilling adventure.
10. Is there medical support during the adult quad bike rides?+
Absolutely. First-aid and emergency support are always available on-site to ensure a safe and worry-free desert experience.
