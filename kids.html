Frequently Asked Questions

1. What’s the minimum age for kids to ride?
Children aged 6 and above can participate under supervision.
2. Are the bikes safe for kids?
Yes! All bikes are speed-limited and tested for maximum safety and control.
3. Do parents need to accompany the kids?
Parents can watch nearby; trained instructors guide every child throughout the ride.
4. What should kids wear?
Comfortable clothing and closed shoes. Safety gear is provided by us.
5. How long does the ride last?
Each session lasts between 15–30 minutes, depending on the package chosen.
6. Are photos and videos included?
Yes, complimentary media is included for every session.
7. Is there medical support available?
Yes, first-aid staff are always on standby for safety assurance.
8. Can siblings ride together?
Yes, we organize rides so siblings can enjoy together under supervision.
9. What happens in bad weather?
In case of strong winds or rain, sessions are rescheduled free of cost.
10. How can I book?
Bookings can be made online, via phone, or directly at our Dubai location.
